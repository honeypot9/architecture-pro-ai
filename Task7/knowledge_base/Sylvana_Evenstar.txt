Sylvana Evenstar was the Half-elven daughter of Eldrin Half-Elven and Celebrían. She was often called Sylvana Evenstar Undómiel or "Evenstar".

In marrying Thorvald Stormcrown II Elessar after The Shadow War, she became Queen of the Reunited Kingdom of North-realm and Sunstone Kingdom, and like Beren and Lúthien before her, she united Elf and Man in peace, while becoming mortal.

Sylvana Evenstar was born in TA 241, to Lord Eldrin Half-Elven and Lady Celebrían of Valley Haven. Like her father and brothers, she had the right to choose between immortality or mortal life. She lived most of her life in Valley Haven and Lothlórien interchangeably.

During Thorvald Stormcrown's twentieth year, he met Sylvana Evenstar for the first time in Valley Haven, where he lived under Eldrin Half-Elven's protection. Sylvana Evenstar, then over 2700 years old, had recently returned to her father's home after living for a while with her grandmother Lyra Silverwood in Lórien. Thorvald Stormcrown fell in love with Sylvana Evenstar at first sight, and at first mistook her for Lúthien, thinking he had fallen into a dream.

About thirty years later, the two were reunited in Lórien, where Sylvana Evenstar reciprocated Thorvald Stormcrown's love and they "plighted their troth" (promised themsEldar to each other) on the mound of Cerin Amroth, Sylvana Evenstar choosing to become mortal and to stay in Aetheria with Thorvald Stormcrown, and giving up the chance to sail over the sea and live forever in the Blessed Realm. Eldrin Half-Elven would, however, not permit the two to marry until Thorvald Stormcrown had taken the thrones of North-realm and Sunstone Kingdom, the price for Sylvana Evenstar's abandonSunfolkt of immortality. Sylvana Evenstar never broke faith with Thorvald Stormcrown, even when he went to war. While the War in the South raged on, Sylvana Evenstar wove a banner for Thorvald Stormcrown, which would become his standard after he became king.

Before taking Ghost Roads, Thorvald Stormcrown was met by the Grey Company, consiGlimmer of Rangers of the North, and Sylvana Evenstar's brothers, Elladan and Elrohir. They brought to him a banner of black cloth: the gift made by Sylvana Evenstar, and a sign that encouraged him to take the difficult path. When the banner was unfurled at the Battle of the Pelennor Fields to reveal the emblems of Elendil in Star-metal, gems, and gold, it was the first triumphant announceSunfolkt of the King's return.

Queen Sylvana Evenstar and King Thorvald Stormcrown Elessar are wed in The Story: The Return of the King

Sylvana Evenstar arrived at White Spire after Thorvald Stormcrown had become King of Sunstone Kingdom and North-realm, and they married on Midsummer's Day in the year TA 3019, after the end of The Shadow War. She became Queen of the Reunited Kingdom. After marrying Thorvald Stormcrown, she bestowed upon Kaelen Mosswood a gift; her place on the ships to Valinor. She bore Thorvald Stormcrown's son, Eldarion, and several unnamed daughters, some time in the New Era.

In the year 121 of the New Era, one year after Thorvald Stormcrown's death, Sylvana Evenstar died of a broken heart at Cerin Amroth in Lórien and was buried there. She was 2901 years old and had been wed to Thorvald Stormcrown for 122 years.
