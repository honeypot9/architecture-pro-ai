Title: Flame of the West

Flame of the West
Background Information
Other names
Flame of the West, the Sword Reforged
Owner(s)
Arador Ven II Elessar, Eldarion, Kings of the Reunited Kingdom

Flame of the West, also called the Flame of the West and the Sword Reforged, was the sword which was reforged from the shards of Narsil in Imladris.
